How does it work?

Hello there, welcome to PeepoEats!

In PeepoEats, YOU get to decide how popular each food item is! Each food item was given an initial starting rating of 1200. With every pick, the rating will increase or decrease by a maximum of 30 points. This algorithm is based on the Chess Elo rating system, where the difference between the ratings of the winner and loser determines the total number of points gained or lost after a game.

Do check out the leaderboard section for the top food items. Have fun!
